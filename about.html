About Us

Overview

We're a couple 'slightly' over 40 that love to travel, both for business and fun.

We're a travel-focused couple who occasionally bring our two children on journeys. We emphasize exploration and self-guided discovery in foreign destinations, though we use professional guides when necessary.

Our Travel Style

We prioritize outdoor activities, particularly hiking and mountain exploration. We maintain moderate budgets while favoring upscale accommodations. We prefer comfort during rest despite being willing to engage in active pursuits during the day.

Dining & Experiences

We embrace culinary tourism, seeking local food experiences and participating in organized food tours across different cities and countries. Food is an essential part of understanding any culture, and we believe in experiencing destinations through their cuisine.

Why We Travel

We're sharing our experiences to help others who'd like to travel with a similar style. We maintain regular employment and treat this platform as a hobby rather than a commercial venture. Our goal is to inspire and inform fellow travelers who appreciate a balance of adventure, comfort, and authentic experiences.

Whether you're planning your first international trip or looking for new destinations to explore, we hope our experiences can help guide your journey. We've learned a lot along the way, and we're excited to share what we've discovered with you.
